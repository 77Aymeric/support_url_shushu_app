Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to help you.

Is the app free?
Yes, the app is entirely free for now. Enjoy all game modes without limits!
Who is ShuShu for?
ShuShu is ideal for beginners and intermediate levels wishing to automate reading and understanding Chinese numbers, a fundamental skill often overlooked.
I have a problem with voice recognition.
Ensure you are in a quiet environment and have granted microphone access. Quality also depends on your phone's speech engine.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes, most features (Reading, Construction, Courses) are accessible without an internet connection.
How do I save my progress?
Your progress is saved locally on your device. We do not collect personal data without your explicit consent.
